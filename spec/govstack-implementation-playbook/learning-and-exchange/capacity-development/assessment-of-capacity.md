# Assessment of capacity

### Assessing the Level of Knowledge

Digital skills are essential, therefore, there is a need to understand the existing eco-system and to conduct a meaningful training skills assessment and analysis in line with identified GovStack practices.&#x20;

If you want to objectively measure knowledge, as a general rule, you begin with a validated measure of maybe ten questions (e.g., something from a final exam on service design). It is suggested to take a look at the 3 lower levels of [Bloom's Taxonomy](https://www.celt.iastate.edu/instructional-strategies/effective-teaching-practices/revised-blooms-taxonomy/) that measure knowledge, comprehension and application.&#x20;

Inventory of the staff and technology is also an important part of the GovStack's capacity building activities. As a starting point, we have identified various ecosystems and assessment frameworks to help us build a skill matrix.&#x20;



**What is the GovStack Skill Matrix** &#x20;

* The skill matrix shows the ‘digital’ skill in question along with its level of mastering versus the stakeholder.&#x20;
* The matrix will be used to determine the need to capacity building and the topic to be addressed as well as the recipient group&#x20;

Below is an illusrtration of how eGov created a IEC program for rollout of their mGramSeva application in Punjab for collection of water bills&#x20;

#### &#x20;PHASE I- IEC ROLL OUT of mGramaSeva app&#x20;

&#x20;The IEC strategy shall be divided into two parts: &#x20;

&#x20;Phase I- It shall mainly be targeting GPWSC members and ensuring they adopt the app. &#x20;

&#x20;Phase II- It shall mainly be targeting Citizens living in the catchment area, so that they begin to make use of the app to pay their water bills. &#x20;

&#x20;**Objectives Phase I**: &#x20;

* To train and sensitise GPWSC committee members and DWSS about mGramSeva app.&#x20;
* To ensure 100% adoption of mGram Seva app by GPWSC members &#x20;
* To strengthen GPWSCs to plan, monitor and operate water supply and sanitation related services for the village community.&#x20;
* To bolster community- based decentralised governance model at panchayat level &#x20;

&#x20;Target Audience: GPWSCs&#x20;

**Overarching Strategy:** The IEC strategic framework is targeted at household, community and district levels.\
&#x20;![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/GetImage (6).png>)![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/GetImage (5).png>)

_**Key Challenges:**_&#x20;

* Resistance on part of the GPWSC Members in adoption of the app  &#x20;
* Lack of Transparency and Accountability &#x20;
* Fiscal Mismanagement&#x20;

&#x20;_**Long Term Goals:**_ &#x20;

* Adoption of mGramSeva app by GPWSC members&#x20;
* Adoption of digital mode of payment for paying water bills by Citizens &#x20;
* LEAVE NO ONE BEHIND&#x20;
* Bolstering Decentralised model of Governance &#x20;

#### &#x20;STRATEGIES TO BE ADOPTED AT THE IMPLEMENTATION LEVEL &#x20;

1. Sensitization cum Workshop for at micro, meso and macro level&#x20;
2. Using audio-visual tools and print & electronic media collaborations for last mile delivery &#x20;
3. Localised and community owned IEC Content &#x20;
4. Convergence for key message dissemination at multiple levels&#x20;
5. Formation of mGram-Seva Volunteer group/Jal Sakhi’s (SHG Group members) ( similar to Swachta Volunteers) for enabling adoption&#x20;

#### &#x20;IEC STRATEGY: MASS EDUCATION &#x20;

1. Use of print media, electronic media and Social Media community &#x20;
2. Community Owned: Street plays and puppet shows &#x20;
3. Posters, pamphlets, hoardings and use of public transport system&#x20;
4. Use of School  children & young adults as change agents&#x20;
5. Involvement of NYK, NCC, NSS, Scouts guide etc. GoGs, ASHA, Aganwari and other  proactive village level functionaries&#x20;
6. Involvement of Religious leaders&#x20;
7. Involvement of SHG Group&#x20;

#### &#x20;IEC STRATEGY: COMMUNICATION&#x20;

1. Poster, Pamphlet, Banner, Hoarding, leaflet and Brochure&#x20;
2. Technical manuals and consultations&#x20;
3. Slogans and display boards&#x20;
4. Formation of WhatsApp groups using field level workers for content disseminations&#x20;
5. Audio and video clips of mass influencers&#x20;
6. Community Radio &#x20;
7. Active involvement of IEC for Content development  as master trainer to approve trainer.&#x20;
8. Involvement of community development specialist for identification of model village panchayats for piloting&#x20;
9. Development of creatives for Social media platforms&#x20;

#### &#x20;IEC STRATEGY: CAPACITY BUILDING &#x20;

1. Training of Master Trainers&#x20;
2. Preparation of GP level Training Plans with Master Trainers &#x20;
